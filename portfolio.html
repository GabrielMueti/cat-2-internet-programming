My Childhood

I was born in a hospital in Nairobi. At the time we lived in Dandora but relocated to Kitui when I turned 2. My early years were filled with playful adventures and learning about the world around me.

Growing up in a nurturing environment helped me develop a strong sense of curiosity and creativity. I enjoyed playing outdoors and learning from the experiences of elders in my neighborhood.

Education

My education journey started in primary school where I first developed a love for learning. I attended St. Francis of Assisi primary school where the teachers described me as curious, which pushed me to explore more.

I attended St. Charles Lwanga. I participated in numerous academic competitions and workshops which led me to discover my passion for technology. Now as a university student, I am pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at Zetech University.

Skills & Talents

I have developed several technical and creative skills including programming and problem-solving. These skills have helped me grow both academically and personally.

One of my biggest strengths is adaptability. I enjoy learning new technologies and staying up to date with the latest advancements in my field.

Future Aspirations

My dream is to become a software developer, specializing in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. I want to build innovative solutions that drive technological advancements.

Beyond my career, I aspire to mentor young people who are passionate about technology. I believe that sharing knowledge and inspiring others is just as important as personal growth.
